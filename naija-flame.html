Our Story

Born on the Streets of Lagos.

NaijaFlame started with one pot of Jollof, one suya stick, and one belief: Nigerian fast food deserves to be legendary. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just real flavour, served fast.

We source local peppers, smoke our suya over open flame, and cook every pot of Jollof like it's going to a party — because in Lagos, every meal is a celebration.
